Anchored

A powerful representation of a black woman adorned with a headdress made of marine life and corals, which intriguingly envelops the upper part of her face. Set against a deep black background, this artwork is a visual spectacle that conveys a profound message. The centrepiece of the artwork is the woman’s face, which is elegantly adorned with a headdress that appears to merge seamlessly with the marine elements. The headdress features an array of intricate details, including corals, fish, and other underwater creatures, which evoke the depths of the ocean and its diverse inhabitants. This fusion of human and marine elements speaks to a connection between humanity and nature, highlighting the intricate interplay of life forms. The use of vibrant colours and meticulous detailing brings the marine life and corals to life, creating a sense of vibrancy and movement within the artwork. Moreover, “Anchored” offers a hidden layer of content that can be revealed through the use of augmented reality (AR) technology. By using the Artivive app, viewers can unlock additional interactive elements and digital surprises embedded within the artwork. This AR experience allows for a deeper exploration of the piece and adds an interactive dimension to the viewing process.

I Knew Him

This digital artwork on acrylic glass features the iconic character Deadpool dressed in a renaissance-style outfit, holding a skull in his outstretched hand. This juxtaposition of the modern and the classic immediately draws the viewer’s attention. In the background of the artwork, a scene unfolds that is rife with symbolism and meaning. Burning US dollar bills allude to themes of consumerism, materialism, and the impermanence of wealth. A figure of a revolutionary branding a flag with the Louis Vuitton symbol suggests a critique of the commercialisation of revolutionary ideals. The inclusion of the Chanel logo and the prominent appearance of the word “Supreme” further emphasise the interplay between high fashion and modern culture. The artwork’s composition and choice of elements create a rich narrative that invites viewers to interpret its meaning on multiple levels. The fusion of Deadpool, a beloved pop culture character, with historical and contemporary symbols underscores the complex relationship between culture, society, and art.

Supreme Skull

This digital artwork on acrylic glass features a vividly colored skull, rendered in rich shades of purple, which immediately grabs the viewer’s attention. The skull serves as a canvas for the repetition of the iconic “Louis Vuitton” brand name, creating a visually intriguing juxtaposition of high fashion and traditional imagery. The repeated text forms a mesmerizing pattern that covers the entirety of the skull’s surface, offering a commentary on consumerism, luxury, and the intersection of commerce and art. At first glance, the artwork presents a bold and stylish representation of contemporary pop culture. However, the depth of the piece becomes even more apparent when using the Artivive app to unlock hidden content. Through augmented reality, viewers are invited to experience an additional layer of meaning within the artwork. This interaction bridges the gap between traditional visual art and modern technology, inviting viewers to engage with the artwork in a dynamic and innovative way.
